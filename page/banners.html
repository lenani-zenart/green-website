Open Source Project

Green Website is a new open-source platform that is designed to promote internet sustainability and ethics by encouraging websites, platforms, and services to adopt responsible online practices. The platform offers a "Green Website" badge to all websites that adhere to specific guidelines aimed at creating a more positive and sustainable online experience.
